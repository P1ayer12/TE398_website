DESCRIPTION

Dante Bonilla is a Chicago born and raised 2nd generation DJ frrom Humboldt Park. Combining the influences of house, hip hop, pop, and throwbacks, he crafts unique and dynamic sets which contain both musical value and technique, turntablism, as well as live sound, graphics, and production.

DJB is your resident party DJ machine, dropping the most obscene beats multiple times a week at cantinas campuswide. Spinning the wax for 8 years, he learned his technique through both family and practice, creating a style rooted in the origins of DJing with a modern flare.
